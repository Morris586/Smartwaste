How It Works

Four steps to a cleaner city

From request to collection — every step is handled automatically by SmartWaste.

01 📝
Submit Request
Fill in your name, phone, address and waste type. Takes under 60 seconds.
02 📍
Auto Geocoding
OpenRouteService converts your address into GPS coordinates instantly.
03 🗺️
Truck Dispatch
Haversine algorithm finds the nearest available truck and assigns it automatically.
04 ✅
Collection Done
Driver collects waste, admin marks complete, driver returns to available.
